Our Goals & Objectives

Rebero Films Ltd. has defined the target market and has differentiated itself by offering a unique and tailored solution to customers needs. Customer needs have been identified to ensure the goals set forth below are achieved:
  • Produce education films based cultural values.
  • Sell Films Packages-Allowing advertisers to campaign on the films we produce.
  • Focus on role models personalities included in our audiovisual presentations.
  • Focusing on cultural documentary style storylines.
  • Develop serial commercial and social storylines - this is storylines over a series of episodes with suspense to keep viewers looking for the next instalment.
  • Involve different players(sponsors) and keep the production house sustained and viable in the long term.
  • Establish national and Regional collaboration channels.

Rwanda, as the member of the East African(EAC) and the Greatest Lakes Region, its highest aim is to become service based economy.

Rebero Films Ltd. want to build on that direction and factor to peg its long-term objectives. The ability to transact freely across and beyond boundaries will drive cross cultural and media co-operation.

The Rwandan audiovisual market has been slow in growth. This is majorly contributed to by poverty (acquiring basic audiovisual equipment), limited knowledge of the sector by customers and lack of a competitive culture in this sector.

Currently in Rwanda,most products and services sell simply because they are available with very little marketing input. Sometime barrier languages can hamper the penetration of audiovisual products since most of the products available on market are in foreign languages. Rebred Films Ltd. aims at reducing that gap.

Advertising in the Rwandan context is fairly basic. This arises from the fact that little is understood on quality audiovisual production and its benefits to the service or product providers.

Objectively, if our products are sold on the benefits to the people of Rwanda and those of the region, as we improve the standards in the industry as a whole, we expect a major turnaround with the use of appealing visuals,developing themes and diverse storyline approaches with long term visibility tailored for specific consumers.
