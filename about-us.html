What we are

Rebero films Ltd. it's a private company created by Rwandans in 2000. The Rwandan audiovisual market has been slow in growth. This is majorly contributed to by poverty (acquiring basic audiovisual equipment), limited knowledge of the sector by customers and lack of a competitive culture in this sector.

Currently in Rwanda,most products and services sell simply because they are available with very little marketing input. Sometime barrier languages can hamper the penetration of audiovisual products since most of the products available on market are in foreign languages. Rebero Films Ltd. aims at reducing that gap.

Our Goals

  • Produce education films based cultural values.
  • Sell Films Packages-Allowing advertisers to campaign on the films we produce.
  • Focus on role models personalities included in our audiovisual presentations.
  • Focusing on cultural documentary style storylines.
  • Demonstrations-making use diverse figures from public life to promote products and services.
  • Develop serial commercial and social storylines - this is storylines over a series of episodes with suspense to keep viewers looking for the next instalment.
  • Involve different players(sponsors) and keep the production house sustained and viable in the long term.
  • Establish national and Regional collaboration channels.
